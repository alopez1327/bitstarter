About us


The Mutable Learning set of tools are part of a larger effort to facilitate online and distance learning. Our company is called iEureka Soluciones en Innovaci√≥n Educativa (iEureka for short) and was created by three university professors.

iEureka started as a repository of freely available educational content that is created by professors throughout Latin America. However, we have seen an increased interest from non-spanish speaking countries and plan to create content for other languages, starting with English. The educational material is meant to be assembled into a single bundle, or learning unit, that can be used by other professors to help their students achieve a particular learning outcome. The learning units can be as simple as a word processing document, a PDF file, a spreadsheet, or as complex as a set of documents that describe a sequence of activities. We currently have over 100 learning units freely available, although most of them are meant for university and high-school students we have some K-12 materials as well.

We are in the process of developing web-based applications that can facilitate the use of the learning units already on our site, and our first effort is the Mutable Learning Assistant.
