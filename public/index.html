Studies have shown that we all learn in different ways, either through concrete experiences or by abstract conceptualization. Some of us are visual learners and some are auditory learners. Thus the question arises, Why not transform and complement the traditional class materials with those learning elements that help us understand and master a particular topic? Why not seek help from our peers in a systematic way?

The Mutable Learning Assistant will help you change the structure or to complement the traditional class materials with any media document that is publicly available. You can add your own notes, and even share this mutable document with your friends. If you so desire, your friends can change the document through collaborative learning.

You can even create a virtual library of these mutable documents, which you can access from your computer or mobile device any time you want.

FAQ

What is Mutable Learning?
  • Mutable Learning is a set of cloud-based tools that allows you to complement your class materials with any learning element that helps you understand and master a particular topic.
  • Any media that you choose tho add to your class materials will then become a virtual book that you can access from your computer or mobile device.
  • We plan to let you manipulate the presentation of your virtual books so you can rearrange and resize each learning element to suit your needs. We will provide some guidelines/templates to facilitate viewing your virtual books on mobiler devices.
How does the service work?
  • You create an account in our servers which will host links to materials that you want to add to your virtual book. You can also upload your own material.
  • The service has a flat fee of $10/year which covers the costs of processing and storage of your virtual documents. You can store as many virtual documents as you like but please note that each account has up to 5 Gbytes of storage.
  • We expect to increase the amount of storage in the future, at no cost to you, but in the mean time if you require more storage please contact us for pricing information.
What about copyright?
You are responsible for linking only to public domain media. If you upload any data into our servers it is assumed that you own the copyright for that material. We will never hold any claims of copyright to your data.
